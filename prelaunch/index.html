School Overview

Maiduguri Capital School is one of Nigeria’s leading pre-tertiary educational institutions, located in the heart of Maiduguri, Opposite Former Assemby Quarters, Gombole Road, P.M.B 1017, Maiduguri - Borno State. The school is known for its academic excellence, modern facilities, and commitment to digital learning.

About Us

Maiduguri Capital School, a premier citadel of learning, began humbly on the 18th of September, 1988, with just three pupils and two teachers. The school graduated its first set of nine pupils in 1991.

In 1993, a secondary section was introduced with 14 pioneer students and six teachers. The first set of graduates from the secondary section completed their studies in 1999.

As of 2025, the school has successfully graduated approximately 18 sets of students, totaling over 6,000 graduates — averaging more than 400 students per year.

Capital School Annex

Due to the increasing demand for enrollment, the Capital School Annex was established. It provides a conducive environment for teaching and learning and houses facilities for conducting national examinations such as WAEC and NECO.

Digitalization & Technology

The school operates many education-related software systems and continues progress toward maximum digitalization.

  • JAMB Mock CBT Software – used to prepare students for UTME exams
  • School in the Box (premium version) – comprehensive cloud-based school management
  • Catch Them Young: Computer Programming Using Python App – coding education and practice
  • Mainafly Memory Game
  • ArithmeticQ - a gamified arithmetic learning app
