About Maiduguri Capital School

Founded: 18th September, 1988 — starting with three pupils and two teachers. The secondary section began in 1993. By 2025 the school has graduated about 18 sets totaling over 6,000 graduates.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To raise smart, confident, and responsible young people who respect their culture and use their knowledge to serve and uplift their communities.

Mission: To provide children with a strong and affordable education that nurtures their intellect, equips them with technological skills, and keeps them grounded in cultural and moral values.
