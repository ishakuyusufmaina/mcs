About Us

Maiduguri Capital School, a premier citadel of learning, began humbly on the 18th of September, 1988, with just three pupils and two teachers. The school graduated its first set of nine pupils in 1991.

In 1993, a secondary section was introduced with 14 pioneer students and six teachers. The first set of graduates from the secondary section completed their studies in 1999.

As of 2025, the school has successfully graduated approximately 18 sets of students, totaling over 6,000 graduates â€” averaging more than 400 students per year.
