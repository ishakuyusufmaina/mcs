About Maiduguri Capital School

Maiduguri Capital School began humbly on 18th September 1988 with just three pupils and two teachers. The school graduated its first set of nine pupils in 1991. In 1993, a secondary section was introduced with 14 pioneer students; the first secondary graduates completed their studies in 1999.

As of 2025 the school has graduated approximately 18 sets — totaling over 6,000 graduates, averaging more than 400 students per year. Today MCS is known for academic excellence, modern facilities, and its commitment to digital learning.

Vision

To raise smart, confident, and responsible young people who respect their culture and use their knowledge to serve and uplift their communities.

Mission

To provide children with a strong and affordable education that nurtures their intellect, equips them with technological skills, and keeps them grounded in cultural and moral values.
