Admission and School Fees

Primary and Nursery Sections

Admission Procedure

  1. Purchase Admission Form: Available at the Head Master’s Office for ₦2,000.
  2. Submit the Completed Form: Return the filled form with required documents and present your ward for the interview.

Age Requirements

  • Crèche: Accepts children below 2 years.
  • Playclass: Child must be 2 years and above.
  • Nursery: Child must be at least 3 years.
  • Primary: Child must be at least 3 years and must have completed Nursery.
Enquiries: Call or WhatsApp the Head Master’s Office: 08061717939

Secondary Section (Junior & Senior Secondary)

Admission Procedure

  1. Buy Admission Form: Obtain from the Principal’s Office for ₦2,000.
  2. Submit the Form: Fill and return the form with the required documents, and bring your ward for the interview.

Requirements

  • JSS 1: Must have completed Primary School and present a Primary School Certificate.
  • SSS 1: Must have BECE result and recent school report card.
Enquiries: Call or WhatsApp the Principal’s Office: 08036410712

Transfer Students/Pupils

Students transferring from another school must present their most recent result/report from their former school.
